Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Ingredients

    400g (14 oz) spaghetti
    4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
    1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
    1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
    Salt to taste
    Freshly ground black pepper to taste
    Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
    Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

    Cook the Pasta
        Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
        Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, according to package instructions.
        Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.

    Prepare the Sauce
        While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
        Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using).
        Sauté until garlic is golden and fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

    Combine Pasta and Sauce
        Add cooked pasta to the skillet.
        Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the garlic oil.
        If the mixture seems dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen it.

    Season and Serve
        Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
        Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
        Serve immediately.

Tips

    For extra flavor, add a squeeze of lemon juice or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes.
    Use high-quality olive oil for the best taste.
    Feel free to add cooked shrimp or grilled chicken for added protein.
